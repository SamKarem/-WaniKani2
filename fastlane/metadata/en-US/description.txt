WaniKani - is a client app for WaniKani.com site. 
It's not official WaniKani app, but it is approved by WaniKani team.

Features:
 - Practice Kanji stroke order.
 - Reorder script.
 - Fast forward script.
 - Ignore button.
 - Smart resizing.
 - Leaderboards and Achievements.
 - Notifications.
 - 3D touch support.
